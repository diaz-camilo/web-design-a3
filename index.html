about

Welcome to Ivy Mae Photography! Meet Ivy Chapel, a fresh talent in the world of photography. Armed with a Bachelor of Arts in Photography and Illustration, Ivy is passionate about translating her skills into captivating visual stories.

Having already dabbled in freelance projects for friends and family, Ivy is now eager to broaden her horizons and work on more professional endeavors. Her heart lies in the realms of fashion and editorial photography, where she thrives on creating stunning, narrative-driven imagery. Despite her focus on these areas, Ivy also has a keen interest in portraiture, adding a versatile edge to her portfolio.

Join Ivy on her journey as she combines her artistic vision with technical skill to capture moments that last a lifetime. Whether you're a fashion enthusiast, a business owner, or someone looking to capture the essence of a moment, Ivy Mae Photography is here to make your vision a reality.
